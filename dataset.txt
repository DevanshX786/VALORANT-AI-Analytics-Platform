The dataset includes matches, agents, and player data from VCT 2021–2025. This was obtained via data scraping at vlr.gg. Each year contains three folders: agents, matches, player stats, and IDs.

The agents folder contains agent pick rates, map pick rates, attacker and defender side win/loss percentage, team pick rates on an agent, and win/loss rate.

The matches folder contains team picks and bans, their economy on each round of a match, their economy stats on a match, players kills performance on other players, players kill stats, maps that were played on a match, the scores from the map, players overview stats, a player kills performance on players and their agent on a specific round, matches scores and their results, a list of abbreviated team names with their full names, the count of the method that occurred for a team for a match they played and its round number.

The player stats folder only contains player stats.

The ids folder contains the ids for the teams, players, tournaments, stages, match types, matches, and games.

The all_ids folder contains all the IDs, and the abbreviated team name with their full name.

Notes

Starting Masters Toronto (VCT 2025), the loadout value in eco_rounds.csv will be missing.

A lot of stats from matches played in China are missing. From what I read online, the Chinese hosted events do not have APIs available for post game stats.

There is a player named “nan”. If you are interested in their data, please make sure to not have the code read their name as null.

There is a team named "TBD". This is not a real team. Either the players were not on a team or their team name was missing at the time the match was played.

There are two teams with the same name but different IDs: Exotic (4964, year 2021) and Exotic (1301, year 2022).

The ID for match types can be null because an ID was not found for it during the scraping. The ID for stages and match types are null if it referring to all the stages and all the match types for a tournament.

The column "Initiated" can contain null values because it is not possible to initialize a pistol round.

The columns "Player", "Enemy", "Player Kills", "Enemy Kills", and "Difference" can contain null values because the name was missing or the player never got first kills and/or never got kills using the Operator and vice versa.

The columns "2k", "3k", "4k", "5k", "1v1", "1v2", "1v3", "1v4", and "1v5" can contain null values because
the player never got a multi-kill elimination or never won in a multi-players match up.

The columns "Team A Overtime Score", "Team B Overtime Score" and "Duration" can contain null values because the match never reached to overtime or the duration was not recorded on the website.

All the columns related to the stats can contain null values because the stats were missing or the player did not encounter situations in a match conductive to recording those specific stats.

The columns "Eliminator", "Eliminated", and "Eliminated Team" can contain null values because the name were missing.

There are two teams with the same abbreviation of TP: Typhoon and Typhone.

Understanding the Data

Tournament ID - The ID for the tournament

Stage ID - The ID for the stage

Match Type ID - The ID for the match type

Match ID - The ID for the match

Game ID - The ID for the map that was played from the match

Pick Rate - How many times an agent has been picked

Total Maps Played - How many times a map has been played

Attacker Side Win Percentage - The percentage of rounds won by the attacking side on that map

Defender Side Win Percentage - The percentage of rounds won by the defending side of that map

Total Wins By Map - How many times the team won with the given agent on that map

Total Loss By Map - How many times the team lost with the given agent on that map

Total Maps Played - The total amount the team played on that map

Loadout Value - The total value of all the weapons, abilities, and shields purchased by all players on the team in a round

Remaining Credits - How much credits a team has after spending

Type - The economy round type (eco, semi-eco, semi-buy, full buy)

Pistol Round - Team won the pistol round

Eco (won) - Team spent 0-5k credits to win a round

$ (Semi-eco) - Team spent 5-10k credits to win a round

$$ (Semi-buy) - Team spent 10-20k credits to win a round

$$$ (Full buy) - Team spent over 20k credits to win a round

Initiated - How many times the team engage a specific economy round (eco, semi-eco, semi-buy, full buy)

Won - How many times the team won a specific economy round (eco, semi-eco, semi-buy, full buy)

Eliminator Team - The team the eliminator is on

Eliminator - The player who eliminated

Eliminator Agent - The agent the eliminator played

Eliminated Team - The team the eliminated is on

Eliminated - The player who got eliminated

Eliminated Agent - The agent the eliminated played

Kills - How many times a player eliminated the specific player on their agent

2k - How many times the player achieved two eliminations

3k - How many times the player achieved three eliminations in a single round

4k - How many times the player achieved four eliminations in a single round

5k - How many times the player achieved five eliminations in a single round

1v1 - How many times the played won against one player from the enemy team when both are the sole players left in a single round

1v2 - How many times the player won against two players from the enemy team when the player is the sole player left on their team in a single round

1v3 - How many times the player won against three players from the enemy team when the player is the sole player left on their team in a single round

1v4 - How many times the player won against four players from the enemy team when the player is the sole player left on their team in a single round

Econ - The economy rating of a player (damage dealt per 1000 credits spent)

Spike Plants - How many times a player planted the spike

Spike Defuse - How many times a player defused the spike

Team Score - The overall score the team accumulated in a match

Team Attacker Score - The score the team accumulated on attacker side in a match

Team Defender Score - The score the team accumulated on defender side in a match

Team Overtime Score - The score the team accumulated during overtime in a match

Duration - How long the match was in hours, minutes, seconds

Rating (R) - Rating the player's performance taking account all the stats available in a match
(https://www.vlr.gg/160667/vlr-gg-player-rating-explained)

Average Combat Score (ACS) - The average combat score over the whole match for the player calculated based on damage, kills, multi-kills and non-damaging assists

Kills - The number of elimination in a match

Deaths - The number of deaths in a a match

Assists - The number of assists in a match

Kills - Deaths (FD) / Kills:Deaths - The kills-to-deaths differential

Kill, Assist, Survive, Trade % (KAST) - % of rounds the player either got a kill, assist, traded a kill or survived

Average Damage Per Round (ADR) - The average damage the player achieved per round

Kills Per Round (KPR) - The average kills the player achieved per round

Assists Per Round (APR) - The average assists the player achieved per round

First Kills Per Round (FKPR) - The average first kills the player achieved per round

First Deaths Per Round (FDPR) - The average first deaths the player achieved per round

Headshot % - % of bullets that were headshots

Clutch Success % - % of clutches the player were able to clutch

Clutches (won/played) - The ratio of won-to-played clutches

Maximum Kills in a Single Map - The maximum elimination the player achieved in a single map

First Kills - How many first elimination the player achieved

First Deaths - How many first deaths the player achieved

Kills - Deaths (FKD) - The first kills-to-deaths differential

Elimination - The amount of times a team has won by eliminating the enemy team

Detonated - The amount of times a team has won by planting the spike and the spike does not get defused by the enemy team

Defuse - The amount of times the team has won by defusing the spike

Time Expiry (No Plant) - The amount of times the team has won when the opposing team does not plant in time

Eliminated - The amount of times the team has lost because they got eliminated

Defuse Failed - The amount of times the team has has lost because they failed to defuse the spike

Detonation Denied - The amount of times the team has lost because the opposing team defused the spike

Time Expiry (Failed to Plant) - The amount of times the team has lost because they failed to plant the spike in time
